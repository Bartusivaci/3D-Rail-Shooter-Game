## INTRODUCTION:

Saving Gargantua is a 3D Rail Shooter game. Your goal is to hit as many enemy ships as possible while avoiding them. 

## HOW TO PLAY:

* WASD Keys to MOVE and ROTATE your ship
* SPACEBAR / LMB to SHOOT
